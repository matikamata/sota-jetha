Review completion

This preview shows how a finished subtitle review would move toward a Jetha review queue. Real submission is not enabled in this public demo.

  1. Reviewer checks and edits the subtitle candidate.
  2. Reviewer prepares a local review package.
  3. In a future enabled workflow, the package can be submitted to a private Jetha review queue.
  4. Jetha or designated human reviewers decide what, if anything, is approved, published, canonical, or sealed.

Submitting is not approval. Approval, publication, and seal remain separate human decisions.
